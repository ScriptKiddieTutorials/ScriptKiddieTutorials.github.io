NTHU IONCamp




Thoughts

During the unforgettable five days of NTHU IONCamp, I met some friends and strong competitors. It was an eye-opening experience for me in the world of competitive programming. I feel like I've improved a whole lot. In the competitions on Day 3 & 5, I ranked 2nd. I could hardly believe it myself.

Even more amazingly, during these five days of the camp, we had to attend several hours of classes every day and compete until 10:30 PM. I wouldn't sleep until 1:00 AM or later, yet I never felt tired because competitive programming is something I love. I absolutely love IONC -- it's literally a paradise for competitive programming. Each night, there were also super exciting competitions. Sadly, tomorrow I have to return to reality.

All in all, this camp allowed me to meet many strong competitors and realize my significant improvement and great potential. On the other hand, it made me aware of my weaknesses, highlighting many areas I need to strengthen or even explore for the first time.
